Yuanzhe Liu

Hi! I am a Master's student in Data Science at the University of Pennsylvania. Previously, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from UW–Madison. I am currently a visiting Research Assistant at UIUC, where I am fortunate to work with Prof. Ismini Lourentzou and Prof. James M. Rehg. At Penn, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Chris Callison-Burch and Prof. Dan Roth.

My research focuses on the capability of embodied agents and applying them for perception, reasoning, and planning in the physical world. My work lies at the intersection of Multimodal Learning (VLMs/MLLMs), Robot Learning (Robotic Foundation Models, VLA), and Deep Reinforcement Learning.
